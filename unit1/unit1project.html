The Front Bottoms

A brief history of the band

The Front Bottoms is an acoustic indie band from New Jersey. They formed in 2006 and have been making music ever since. The group was created by two childhood friends, Brian Sella (vocals and lead Guitar) and Mattthew Uychich (drums).
Though Brian and Matthew are the foundation of the band, they have had two previous members leave and often have other musicians join them on tour.

Current Members:
  • Brian Sella - Lead Vocals, Lead Guitar (2006-Present)
  • Matthew Uychich - Drums (2006-Present)
Past Members:

Albums and EPs

In the band's 16 year history, all of their songs have been written and composed almost entirely by Brian and Matthew. The only outside input they have recieved is when trying to add an instrument they aren't experienced with to a track - such as their songs that include trumpet.
In total, the band has released seven studio albums and 10 Eps (with an 11th being released September 2nd).
