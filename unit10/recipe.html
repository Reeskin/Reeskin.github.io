Meatloaf Recipe

Ingredient Amount Picture of Ingredients
90%/10% Ground Beef 1 Pound
80%/20% Ground Beef 1 Pound
White Onion 1/2 Cup (Finely Chopped)
Eggs 2 Eggs
Maple Syrup 1 Cup
Ketchup 1 Cup
Yellow Mustard 2 Tablespoons
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Begin making the sauce by mixing together the syrup, ketchup, and mustard. You may add more or less ingredients to the sauce to your taste. Put the sauce mixture to the side and combine all other ingredients in to a separate bowl. Pour half of the sauce in with your ingredients. It is easiest to mix these ingredients by hand, but be sure to wash them before and after! Next, move your now mixed ingredients into an oiled baking pan or skillet - be sure to spread the mixture evenly across the pan. After your mixture is spread, poke holes in rows along the mixture and then pour the rest of your leftover sauce along the top, making sure the top is covered and the holes begin to fill. Place your baking pan in the oven for one hour. Remove the meatloaf, let cool, and enjoy!
