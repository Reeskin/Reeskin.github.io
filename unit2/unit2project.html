Ants

Ants are everywhere, whether you see them or not. Though they are seen as a pest to most, you might as well get yourself acquanted with them as they are all aorund you. Here are some species that you may or may not have heard of:

  • Common Species:
    1. Carpenter Ant
    2. Fire Ant
    3. Pavement Ant
  • Uncommon Species:
    1. Giant Amazonian Ant
    2. Fish Hook Ant
    3. T-Rex Ant
More Species

Ants are omnivors that have a fairly simple diet. Being omnivores, they will eat whatever they possibly can, so long as they are able to digest it. Some of the more common things in their diet include:

  • Food dropped by humans
  • Fruits & Veggies
  • Dead Animals & Bugs
More Diet Information

Ants may be small in stature, but they are capable of much more than people realize. Comparitivly, if humans were able to accomplish the same feats as ants, we would be actual superheros (when their size is taken into account). Some of these ant abilities are:

  • Ants are one of the strongest creature relative to their size. A single ant can carry 50 times its own bodyweight. This would be like the average man casually lifting 9,500 pounds.
  • The bullet ant has the most painful sting of any insect according to the Schmidt Sting Index - an index built around a man letting insects bite/sting him and rating them on an exponential scale.
  • Ants are the longest living insects. Some species of ant queens are able to stay alive up to 30 years.
